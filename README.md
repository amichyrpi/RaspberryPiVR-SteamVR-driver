# RaspberryPiVR SteamVR driver

Here is the guide that will explain to you how to install and configure the steamvr driver

1: First you need to install the SteamVR driver and unzip it with WinRAR for example, it is in the releases section.

2: secondly you have to put the driver in the steamvr driver folder it is located here C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\SteamVR\drivers once put it should look like this ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cec5ed36-0ffb-4b68-8ac7-2fa5363ad184)

3: lately to make it work with see vr headset DIY you will have to change parameters in steamvr the first parameter to change is the display of the vr view you will have to click on display the vr view, a window will appear in this window you should go to menu

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/10be088e-0e5c-4af3-bbd7-6106b09b983c)

once in menu you will have to click on two eyes in the window that appears

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5adc4f99-8076-4321-9f4a-7b9c9024b12a)

when you had done that you will have to do two last things 1: put the vr view window on the screen of your vr headset connected to your pc 2: click on "full screen" at the top right of the window

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7681f36e-c6dd-44ca-baa6-e9f3cc85a891)

once this last thing is done you will have to click on the large red rectangle on your main screen and there you have it your DIY vr headset is finished
